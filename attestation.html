At Al Mostashar Al Dhaki, we provide professional and reliable certificate attestation services to ensure your documents are legally recognised for use both locally and internationally. Attestation is a mandatory process for validating the authenticity of certificates, and we make this complex procedure simple, efficient, and stress-free.

Educational
Certificates

Degree certificates, diplomas, transcripts, and other academic documents required for employment, higher education, or migration.

Personal

Birth certificates, marriage certificates, divorce decrees, and other personal documents for legal or immigration purposes.

Commercial

Trade licenses, Article of Association, and other business-related documents for international transactions.

Our Process Covers

Verification and pre-check of documents for accuracy.
Coordination with government authorities, embassies, and consulates.
Attestation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and other relevant bodies.
Delivery of fully attested documents within agreed timelines.
